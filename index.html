Recommended reading of the month

J.R.R. Tolkien, full name John Ronald Reuel Tolkien, was an English writer, poet, philologist, and university professor, best known as the author of the high fantasy works "The Hobbit," "The Lord of the Rings," and "The Silmarillion." He was born on January 3, 1892, in Bloemfontein, South Africa, and died on September 2, 1973, in Bournemouth, England.
